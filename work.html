Internship Resources


    Internships are highly valuable for students in getting meaningful experience in your undergraduate career, from companies or organizations typically involved with educational institutions. Especially in neuroscience, being exposed to other people in the field, as well as the connections made, is great in getting a look into and starting up your post-educational career.


    Below is a list of resources that can help you find neuroscience internships, as well as links to information great in acquiring those internships.


  • PathwaysToScience.org
    • This is a great resource for finding internships across the country, with opportunities to apply no matter which stage of education you are at - highschool, undergraduate, post-graduate.
  • National Science Foundation
    • Although the site is broader in scope, referencing Biological Sciences, it can be specified for neuroscience internships. However, the best part about this website is the contact information being provided for program coordinators, to get a step in the door as well as more information about the program.
